About Me

Welcome to my website. My name is Eric Bella. I am a beginning software developer. I graduated from Purdue University in Information Systems a while back. I spent some time substitute teaching, working at Walmart, and working at Cracker Barrell while I was attempting to pursue an IT Job. As I was unable to find a job in IT I took on a position as an Administrative Assistant for the Arkansas Department of Environmental Quality where I worked for 10 years. I decided to come back to my home state of Indiana and found out about the software boot camp with Eleven Fifty Academy. After going through this training I am looking for a software development position in which I may apply the skills I have to help a growing organization or business. I will be learning new skills both on the job and in my free time to ensure that I will be the best software developer I can be.
